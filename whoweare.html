About Makos Infotech

Makos Infotech Private Limited was incorporated on 08th December 2020 with a vision to help individuals and institutes achieve excellence in their day-to-day routine.

At Makos Infotech, we achieve our goals by developing robust IT systems that help our customers build effective transactions. We make software applications that provide solutions beyond administration, documentation, tracking, reporting and automation.

We accomplish our vision through innovation in our products that fulfill and adapt to the dynamic customer needs. We also strive to become an integral part of our customer’s routine and help them achieve their desired outcomes.

We move forward with the intention of the organization’s growth, implementing and fulfilling the needs and demands of our users. We fuel our desire to deliver excellence with efficiency and dedication.

We strive to provide our users with the best assistance with better experience in various areas of Information Technology.
