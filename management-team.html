Our Roots

Mr. Ashokk Remi John

Mr. Ashokk Remi John is a youthful, hardworking businessman with many skills. He received the Best Business Leader honor at the Internet 2.0 conference in Dubai. Mr. John has accumulated significant expertise and knowledge via his engagement in different facets of business and his roles as CEO and Innovation Ambassador.

Mr. Ashokk Remi John, an entrepreneur from the first generation who is passionate about creating reliable IT systems and software applications, created Makos Infotech Private Limited on December 8th, 2020.

He aims to assist businesses and communities in enhancing their greatness through technology. He aims to create a reputable organization using technology to give the most outstanding business solutions and lifestyles.
